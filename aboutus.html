Welcome to Ratoath Sports Injury Centre

Established 2004 by sports therapist Sarah Daly. Located in Unit 3, The Village Centre, Ratoath, this centre has been evolving and developing over the past 10 years to provide our patients with the latest prevention and rehabilitation care for elite and recreational athletes. We also assist with Diet & Nutrition. The centre has recently expanded to meet the demands of our patients and to offer the latest rehabilitation services to facilitate the healing process. We offer Sports Therapy, Physical Therapy and Acupuncture to name but a few. We also have Cyro-Spa facilities and the revolutionary Alter G Alterg Treadmill . Thank you for visitng our website, we hope to be of assistance to you soon.
